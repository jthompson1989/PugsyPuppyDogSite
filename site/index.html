About Pugsy

Pugsy is a wonderful dog who brings joy to everyone around. He found his way into our lives back in 2018 when he was a 14 year old dog at the local shelter in Golden Valley. He was a very quiet and shy dog, but he eventually got comfortable with us and also a bit more bossy about getting snacks. He did love to play with his toys, but as time went on he started to slow down a bit. Now the only toy he enjoys is his treat puzzle, which he loves to solve. He also loves to go on walks and sniff everything he can find.
